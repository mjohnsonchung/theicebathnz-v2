Why the Ice Bath Chiller

Cold, precisely.

Purpose-built for serious cold therapy. The Ice Bath Chiller pairs with your existing tub — or add it to any setup — and delivers exact, consistent temperatures without the hassle of ice.

Cools to 3°C

Gets your water down to 3°C (37°F) — the sweet spot for serious cold therapy. Self-priming circulation pump keeps the temperature consistent throughout.

1 HP Motor

Industrial-grade 1 HP motor built for daily use. Reliable, powerful, and engineered to last — the same quality components used in commercial settings.

Any Tub, Any Setup

Universal connectivity — works with stock baths, inflatable pods, barrels, or timber tubs. If it holds water, the Ice Bath Chiller cools it.

UV + Particle Filtration

Internal UV water filtration and a particle filter keep the water clean between sessions. Less maintenance, more plunges.
