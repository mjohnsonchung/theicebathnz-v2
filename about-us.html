01 / Origin

We built what
we couldn't find.

The Ice Bath NZ started in Auckland because we were athletes who'd tried everything on the market. Inflatable tubs that leaked. Cheap imports that took three months to arrive. Equipment that looked the part but fell apart within a season.

We weren't prepared to compromise our recovery with gear that didn't match our standards. So we built our own — starting from the material up, with G90 galvanised steel, built for durability and designed for New Zealand conditions.

to last.

02 / For whom

For the seriously
committed.

Every product we make is designed for the person who treats recovery as seriously as training. Athletes, coaches, weekend warriors, biohackers, and high-performers who've learned that what you do after the session matters as much as the session itself.

Cold immersion isn't a trend in this community — it's a non-negotiable daily ritual. We make the equipment worthy of that commitment.

03 / How we work

Direct. Local.
No middlemen.

We operate out of Auckland and handle everything ourselves — design, testing, fulfilment, and support. No warehouses in Sydney. No six-week shipping windows. Direct-to-door delivery across New Zealand, backed by a team that answers the phone.

When you invest in one of our products, you're buying from the people who built it. That means faster support, better advice, and accountability that a distributor model can't give you.
